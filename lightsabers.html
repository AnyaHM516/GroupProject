lightsabers

There are hundreds of different and unique lightsabers in the Star Wars universe. From different colors to different handles and even multiple blades. Here's our complete guide on lightsabers.

colours

There are several different colours of lightsaber that we have seen. The most common for Jedi are green and blue, with the Sith using red.

handles

All Jedi make their own lightsabers, so the handles of their lightsabers can reflect who they are and where they come from.

blades

Not all lightsabers have just one blade. Some have two, some act like staffs and others have cross bars.

some examples

Rey's Lightsaber
Colour: Yellow
User: Rey
Features: Rey's lightsaber was made from her old staff, and the hilt has the same design as the hilt of the staff. There is cloth around the handle that matches the sort of cloths she wears throughout the movie. The colour of Rey's lightsaber could have several meanings, one theory is that it represents a balance between good (green/blue) and bad (red). Another suggests it reresents ancient jedi who defended the temples, and implies her role of rebuilding the jedi now that she is the last one left.

Anakins's Lightsaber
Colour: Blue
User: Anakin Skywalker
Features: Anakin made his own lightsaber during his training with Obi Wan Kenobi. However, after turning to the Dark Side and dueling his former master, Obi Wan took the lightsaber from him. Darth Vader would go on to use a red lightsaber after this, and the original would be passed on to Anakin's son, Luke.

Mace Windu's Lightsaber
Colour: Purple
User: Mace Windu
Features: Mace Windu is a jedi but unlike most others who use green or blue, he is the only person in the movies to use a purple lightsaber. This is literally just because Samuel L Jackson asked for one.

Kylo Ren's Lightsaber
Colour: Red
User: Kylo Ren/Ben Solo
Features: Kylo Ren has a red lightsaber like most Sith, but his has a crossbar on the handle with two mini blades coming out of it. The blade itself is also less clean than most lightsaber, making a roaring sound and looking less stable. This instability reflects how unsure of his own purpose he is, as well as referencing the lack of guidance he has had to teach him the ways of the force.

Darth Maul's Lightsaber
Colour: Red
User: Darth Maul
Features: Darth Maul is a character from the prequel movies. While he is not the only character to have a double-bladed lightsaber, he was the first on screen character introduced with one. The single staff can also detach into two seperate regular lightsabers.
