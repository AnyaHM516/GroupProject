the jedi

The Star Wars Universe is centered around the Jedi. These are characters that have the ability to use the Force and belong to an pseudo-religous order which upholds peace, democracy and harmony amongst the various planets and political actors in the galaxy.

the force

The Force is something that is introduced in the Star Wars and forms the foundation for many of the plots. It can best be described as the 'magic' of this universe, giving it's users the ability to push and pull objects, use lightning, sense important developments elsewhere in the universe and even communicate telepathically. While all living people and creatures in the Star Wars universe have the Force in them, only a select few have the ability to consciously sense and manipulate it. The Jedi are all force-users, but only use the 'light' side of this, found through a meditative mental state of peace and calm. While they use it to fight, some uses are forbidden such as choking others and using lightning.

beliefs

The jedi exclusively follow the light side of the force, using their powers for benevolent and peace-creating means. They often are defenders of the downtrodden, and are politically affiliated to democratic galactic orders such as the Republic. While they are not pacifists, they believe in the pursuit of peace and harmony amonst all living beings in the galaxy.

history

The Jedi are a religious order of force-users dating back thousands of years who stood to uphold peace and justice. The are often military leaders for the republic, but maintain working towards peace and unity in the galaxy. The built a temple on the planet of Coruscant where young jedi, called Padawans, could train. The turn of Anakin Skywalker to the dark side also lead to the destruction of this temple and with it, the illimination of all jedi. Years later his son, Luke Skywalker would rebuild this order after Darth Vader's death.

notable characters

The Skywalker family, including Anakin and Luke are central characters to the story that the Star Wars universe tells. Both are trained in part by Obi Wan Kenobi, who protects and hides Luke and Leia following Anakin's turn to the dark side. Other notable jedi include Yoda, Qui-Gon Jinn, Rey and Grogu (aka Baby Yoda).
